Upcoming: Grand Prix USA

Max Verstappen for Red Bull: Max Verstappen was able to clinch the Formula 1 world championship title in 2021 and 2022. Last season, he was so dominant that runner-up Charles Leclerc only had hope of overall victory at the beginning of the season. Only marginal changes were made to Red Bull's car in contrast to the 2022 season. Verstappen, like his team partner Sergio Perez, can therefore fall back on the familiar. Verstappen has already proven that the car suits him. Verstappen was also in good shape in the first tests. He is therefore rightly the top favorite in the Formula 1 World Champion odds.
Charles Leclerc for Ferrari: Last season, Charles Leclerc still managed to finish second in the Formula 1 drivers' standings for Ferrari - an achievement that can be built on. Ferrari has made a number of improvements to its SF-23, the successor to the F1-75, which is why the team is optimistic going into the season. Leclerc is also considered by the bookmakers to have a realistic chance of winning the world championship title at fairly low odds.
Luis Hamilton and George Russel for Mercedes: With Lewis Hamilton and George Russell, Mercedes is entering the race with the same drivers as in the previous season. Hamilton only finished 6th in the drivers' standings last season, but the seven-time world champion is always a force to be reckoned with. The problem with the bouncing Silver Arrow of the previous season is said to have been solved, so nothing stands in the way of a repeat of earlier successes. Hamilton is undoubtedly one of the best drivers in the field.
