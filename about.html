Formel 1: More than a sport

Formula 1 is the pinnacle of open-wheel motorsport. It's a high-speed racing championship that features cutting-edge, aerodynamic cars, world-class drivers, and a global series of races known as Grands Prix. In F1, teams like Mercedes, Ferrari, and Red Bull Racing design and build their own cars, pushing the limits of technology and engineering. The season concludes with the crowning of the World Drivers' Champion and the Constructors' Champion, making Formula 1 a thrilling blend of speed, skill, and innovation.
