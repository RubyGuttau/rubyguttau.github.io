MacLaren vs. Ferrari

MacLaren is currently marching from one podium place to the next. The team is riding a wave of success and is hurtling forward in the world championship standings. It seems to be only a matter of time until Aston Martin's fourth place is within their grasp. "It's doable. I think so," said Lando Norris optimistically. It's likely to happen as early as the upcoming weekend in Austin. But that's not all. The McLaren drivers have a taste for victory and are even eyeing a move further ahead towards Maranello. Ferrari currently holds the third position and is also hoping to catch up to Mercedes.
